ὣς ἔφατ᾽

(Hom. Il. 1.43)

(So he said)

ἔφατ᾽: contracted form of ἔφατο, imperfect middle 3rd sg of the verb φημί ("I say, I speak"). The prefix ἔ- (called "augment") indicates that the verb is in the past, the suffix -το is the ending used for 3rd person sg in the historical tenses/aspects and middle voice.

There is a debate whether the augment was originally a separate particle, of if it started since the beginning as a past tense marker prefixed to the verb (Willi 2018, Origins of the Greek verb, ch.7, CUP).
